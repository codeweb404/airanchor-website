Our Story

Team AirAnchor

what we're all about

Team AirAnchor is a group of passionate STEM students united by the Conrad Challenge and driven by a shared vision of innovation. Although we share a common love for STEM, each member brings a unique perspective and skill set, from aerospace and computing to environmental science and 3D printing. Together, we combine these strengths to tackle complex challenges and create something truly transformative. After much exploration, we identified atmospheric observation as the perfect avenue to harness our collective talents, leading to the development of our innovative weather balloon, AirAnchor.

Our Vision

our mission and beyond

Accurate atmospheric observation is essential for daily life, yet errors in measurement can result in flawed forecasts and dangerous consequences. These challenges inspire our dream of a future where precise observations lead to accurate forecasts, scientific breakthroughs, and safer, more predictable weather for everyone.

Team AirAnchor is committed to making this vision a reality. Through our uniquely diverse skills, shared determination, and innovative spirit, we aim to break new ground in atmospheric observation, revolutionizing the field and ensuring a safer, smarter future.
